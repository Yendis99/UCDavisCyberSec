Additional Details

Networking and Cybersecurity

Issued by UC Davis Division of Continuing and Professional Education

The earner of this badge has a demonstrated understanding of system administration and cybersecurity and has completed intensive training in network security monitoring, defensive security, and penetration testing. Program instructional time is 250 hours with up to an additional 500 hours on projects.

Earning Criteria

  • Complete the Networking and Cybersecurity course of UCD CPE Cybersecurity Bootcamp including completion with satisfactory score of all assignments, projects and supplemental activities, and has attended all required classes (95% attendance).
  • Pass projects and assignments including: Secure, configure, and harden servers. System administration. Automate security tasks. Monitor network traffic. Packet analysis. Analyze network tracing. Implement firewall policies. Traffic logs and alerts. Investigate network attack, application attack, and email attack incidents. Collect, preserve, analyze, and report forensic evidence. Penetration tests. Enumerate and scan networks. Manually identify and exploit vulnerabilities and construct payloads.
